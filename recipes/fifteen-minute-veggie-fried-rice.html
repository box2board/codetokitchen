15-Minute Veggie Fried Rice

3–4 servings • Prep: 15 min • Cook: 0 min • Total: 15 min

Use day-old rice for best texture; freezer veggies keep it weeknight-easy.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (day-old preferred)
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Pinch salt + pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add eggs, scramble, and remove.
  2. Add remaining oil. Stir-fry frozen veggies 3–4 minutes.
  3. Add rice, breaking up clumps; stir-fry until heated through.
  4. Return eggs to pan. Season with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Top with green onions and serve.

Notes & Substitutions

Add cooked chicken or shrimp for extra protein. Swap soy sauce for tamari if needed.

AI Twist

Stir in 1 tbsp kimchi or chili crisp for a savory, spicy boost.
