Kitchen Tips

Shortcuts, swaps, and prep tactics that keep weeknights calm.

  • Batch prep aromatics (onion, garlic, herbs) and freeze in tablespoon portions.
  • Keep a “pantry paste” jar: mix tomato paste with olive oil, garlic, and oregano for quick sauces.
  • Use sheet pans for two-zone cooking: proteins on one side, veggies on the other.
  • Finish dishes with acid (lemon, vinegar) to brighten flavors without extra salt.
