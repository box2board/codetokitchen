Sheet-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken

Chicken

Roasted thighs with potatoes & green beans—tangy, garlicky, weeknight-easy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Baby potatoes
  • Green beans + carrots
  • Lemon, garlic, herbs

Instructions

  1. Season chicken + veggies.
  2. Roast at 425°F until crisp.
  3. Finish with lemon juice.
